Food & Beverage

There are many challenges for those that operate, design, and integrate food and beverage manufacturing facilities. FDA regulations, food safety, budget constraints, personnel safety, operational issues, security, and staffing make selecting the right partners and system a critical part of the decision-making process. The years of experience that the experts at GWA Industrial Automation have in supporting end users, integrators and OEMs in the food and beverage market has allowed us to gain extensive technical knowledge to determine the best results. GWA is here to provide solutions to keep your plant operational and secure. We want to put our experience to work for you!

Some of the ways we are able to help our food and beverage customers are by providing solutions that:

• Increase plant productivity by improving utilization, optimizing yields and reducing energy costs

• Visualize your process system so you can make intelligent operational decisions.

• Take advantage of mobile devices to allow your team to be more efficient from where they are.

• Control your processes with state-of-the-art systems that will provide years of operation.

• Safety scanners for protection of personnel from hazardous locations

• Provide Ethernet Network High Availability

This is only a small sampling of the products and services that we can provide you. Please contact us so that we can provide the custom solutions to fit you and your plant’s specific needs.
